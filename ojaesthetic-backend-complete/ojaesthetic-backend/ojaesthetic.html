About the artist

Art that breathes

"I paint feelings, not just subjects."

Hello, I'm Oja — a self-taught artist who believes every canvas holds a universe waiting to unfold. My work lives at the intersection of emotion, texture, and the quiet language of color.

From blooming botanicals to intimate portraits, each piece is an invitation to slow down, feel deeply, and find beauty in the ephemeral.
